ABOUT MINGLIFY

We are an open community for UPC students (current and feature ones) which aims to create a safe place for anyone who wants to find their gang, a new team for an exciting idea or assignment or for anyone who doesn't already feel like tinkering but has a desire for socializing. The goal of this project is to connect people through their ideas but more importantly, increase engangement of those who might feel insecure or unexcited by tech. Just drop by to join an existing group, invite someone who you would like to join or propose whatever project you have in mind. Let's create a vibrant community ir our university, for everyone!
